// The Outlaw-o-Meter™

Your design deserves
a fighting chance.

You used your imagination. You built something from nothing. The last thing you should have to deal with is a silent takedown three days after listing — no warning, no explanation, just gone. Strike on your account. Maybe worse.

The Outlaw-o-Meter scans every design against DMCA records, USPTO trademark databases, and platform-specific enforcement patterns before it ever goes live. It tells you where you stand, why something is flagged if it is, and what you can adjust to give your design the best possible shot at staying up and selling.

No design is ever 100% bulletproof — we'll never claim otherwise. What we can do is make sure you walk in informed instead of blindsided.
